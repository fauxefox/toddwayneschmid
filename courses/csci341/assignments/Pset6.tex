\begin{problem}
    [Cats \(>\) Dogs]
    Let \(A = \{c, a, t, d, o, g\}\). 
    Design a counter automaton with a state \(x\) that accepts the language \(L_{cat}\) of all words \(w \in A^*\) such that the string "\(cat\)" appears in \(w\) more times than "\(dog\)" appears in \(w\).
